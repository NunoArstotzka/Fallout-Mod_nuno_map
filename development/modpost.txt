[h1]Features[/h1]
A unique custom map that turns North America into a unique playing experience, modelling the potential changes the Great War may have brought.

Many countries from Fallout lore, and several more 'fantasy' nations to help provide more interesting gameplay across all of the wasteland.

[h1]Troubleshooting[/h1]
If the mod isn't loading correctly as a Steam Mod, I recommend you download the files from the Github and follow the instructions there. 
That will allow you to run the mod as a local mod. Unfortunately this issue is linked to the launcher, so it is up to Paradox to fix it.
